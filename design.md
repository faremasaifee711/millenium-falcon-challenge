## Core entities: 

- Planets
- Routes: map containing the list of all planets in the galaxy, and the number of days it takes to go from one to the other
- Fuel Requirements:
    - full tank capacity x: fuel remaining for number of x days to travel
    - jump : required 1 day fuel, fills the tank back to its maximum capacity
- Chances of capture:
    - 10% : arrive on planet the day hunters are expected to arrive
    - 10%: : refuels on planet the day hunters are expected to arrive
